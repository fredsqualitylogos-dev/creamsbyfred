About the redesign

Creams Café is known for colourful desserts and a fun customer experience. This redesign focuses on making the website easier to use, especially for mobile users who want to quickly find menu items, offers and store information.

Mobile friendly

The layout adapts to phones, tablets and laptops using CSS Grid, Flexbox and media queries.

Accessible design

Clear headings, readable contrast, alt text and labelled form controls support a wider range of users.

Customer focused

The site gives users useful content instead of placeholder text, helping them make a decision quickly.
